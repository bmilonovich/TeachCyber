Unit 7 Vocabulary


Vulnerability Known gaps or weaknesses in a system.

Risk The potential of loss or harm related to technical infrastructure or the use of technology within an organization.

Threat Agent An individual or group that is capable of carrying out a particular threat. They are characterized by their resources, capabilities/techniques, motivations, and aversion to risk.
Threat Something that has the potential to cause harm. It can be intentional or unintentional. It can originate from a mistake, a natural disaster, or an adversary seeking to cause harm.

Natural Threat An act of nature; for example, earthquakes, floods, forest fires

Unintentional Threat Attributed to human error; for example, leaving a server room unlocked, programming errors, improper settings..
Intentional Threat Intentional acts; for example, malicious code, theft of login credentials, tampering with hardware.
